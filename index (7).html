✦ Our Mission
Why We Reach
for the Stars

The ACC Space & Astrophysics Club brings together students passionate about the universe — hosting lectures, telescope sessions, research discussions, and science competitions.

🔭

Observation Nights

Regular stargazing sessions with telescopes and expert guidance on celestial events and phenomena.

🚀

Research & Projects

Student-led astrophysics research — from exoplanet transit studies to cosmological modelling projects.

🌌

Workshops & Seminars

Guest lectures, NASA resources, and deep-dive physics workshops to strengthen astronomical knowledge.

🏆

Olympiads & Competitions

Preparing members for national and international astronomy olympiads and science fairs.
